About Fanfinity

Fanfinity is a fan-based merchandise website designed for categories like Anime, Music, Movies & Series, and Gaming. It is built to showcase a modern shopping platform experience with product browsing, cart, wishlist, orders, profile handling, and payment flow.

This website is created as a college academic project to demonstrate frontend, backend, database, and full-stack development concepts in a practical way.

Account & Profile

Users can manage their account, login details, profile information, and personal preferences through the Fanfinity platform.

Orders

Fanfinity includes order placement, order history, delivery tracking, and previously delivered products as part of the shopping experience.

Payments

The platform demonstrates payment methods such as UPI and Cash on Delivery to simulate a real e-commerce order flow.

Shipping

Shipping-related details such as delivery timelines, order summaries, and product dispatch flow are shown in a simple structured manner.

Returns

Return and replacement concepts are included to represent how post-delivery support can be handled in an online shopping platform.

Help & Support

This page works as a common support and information page for the footer links so users can understand the purpose and features of Fanfinity.

Important Information

This website is developed as a college academic project. All products, names, images, and content used on this platform are for educational and demonstration purposes only.

Fanfinity is not intended to function as a real commercial shopping platform.
